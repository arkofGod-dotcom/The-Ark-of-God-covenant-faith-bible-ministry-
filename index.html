About Us

The Ark of God Covenant Faith Bible Ministry is a prophetic and apostolic commission raised by God to prepare men and women for the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ. We are a ministry committed to raising kingdom giants, restoring destinies, and equipping believers for victorious living through the power of the Word and the Holy Spirit.

Our mandate is to transform lives through sound teaching, intense prayer, and divine encounters. We believe in the operation of the supernatural, healing, deliverance, and the prophetic move of God.

Our Annual Convention comes up every March, drawing believers from different regions into a season of revival, impartation, and spiritual awakening.

About the Founder

Pastor Dr. E.A Abioye is a seasoned Prophet of God with over 25 years of impactful ministry. He is a man of prayer, deeply anointed in healing, teaching, and prophetic operations.

His ministry is marked by undeniable manifestations of God's power, bringing healing to the sick, direction to the lost, and restoration to broken destinies. Through divine insight and revelation, he has guided countless individuals into their God-ordained purpose.

He carries a strong apostolic and prophetic grace, raising men of fire who are committed to kingdom advancement.
