About Us

The Ark of God Covenant Faith Bible Ministry is a prophetic and apostolic commission raised by God to prepare people for the second coming of Christ, raising destinies for kingdom purposes and equipping believers for victorious living.

We are committed to raising men of fire through sound teaching, intense prayer, and the demonstration of the power of the Holy Spirit. This ministry is a place of transformation, restoration, and divine encounters.

Our mandate is to restore lives, build kingdom giants, and spread the gospel across nations through the operation of the supernatural.

Annual Convention: Every March

Meet the Founder

Pastor Dr E.A Abioye is a seasoned Prophet of God with over 25 years of ministry experience. He is a man of prayer, deeply anointed in healing, teaching, and prophetic operations.

His ministry is marked by undeniable manifestations of God’s power—bringing healing, restoration, and divine direction to countless lives.

He carries a strong apostolic and prophetic grace, raising men of power and kingdom relevance across nations.
