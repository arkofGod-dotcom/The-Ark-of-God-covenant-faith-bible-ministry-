About Us

THE ARK OF GOD COVENANT FAITH BIBLE MINISTRY is a divine commission raised by God to restore lives, revive destinies, and raise a generation that will walk in power, truth, and kingdom dominion.

We are a prophetic and apostolic ministry committed to teaching the undiluted Word of God, demonstrating the power of the Holy Spirit, and establishing believers in righteousness, faith, and spiritual authority.

This ministry is a place of transformation where burdens are lifted, yokes are broken, and lives are aligned with God’s divine purpose. Through intense prayers, sound teaching, and prophetic impartation, we raise kingdom giants who will influence their world for Christ.

Our mandate is clear:
• To raise men of fire
• To restore broken destinies
• To build a people of power and holiness
• To spread the gospel across nations

Our Annual Convention comes up every March.

Meet the Pastor

Pastor Dr E.A Abioye is a seasoned servant of God, a prophetic voice, and a passionate teacher of the Word, called with a divine mandate to raise men into spiritual maturity, power, and kingdom relevance.
